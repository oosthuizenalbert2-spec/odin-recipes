Lasagna Recipe

This lasagna recipe is a delicious, creamy blend of flavours which will impress any fellow food enthusiast

Ingredients

  • lasagna noodles
  • tomatoes
  • ground beef
  • ricotta cheese
  • onion
  • eggs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large pot, cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  3. In a large skillet, cook and stir ground beef until brown. Add chopped onions and cook until translucent. Stir in tomatoes, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with beaten eggs.
  5. To assemble, spread a layer of meat sauce in the bottom of a baking dish. Layer with noodles, ricotta mixture, and mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers, and top with remaining meat sauce and cheese.
  6. Bake in preheated oven for 30 minutes. Cool for 10 minutes before serving.
